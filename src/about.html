Creagoo is a mobile developer.

My name is Dybka Daniil and I develop games for people. I have other projects as well, which you can find out about at my site .

Mobile projects

I decided to start developing mobile projects out of interest. The first developed project — Babka On The Hunt.

This game is made in the 2D platformer genre because of simplicity of project development. By the way, the genre 2D is chosen not because of the simplicity of development, but rather the simplicity of perception. Yes, and it often turns out, that the games come out undemanding and better optimized.

Creagoo Development

Unfortunately, but in 2023 I do not plan to develop new projects and publish them under the name Creagoo.

To a greater extent, this is due to employment and lack of interest. But I'm still interested in helping people develop projects.

How Creagoo was born

I came up with my name Creagoo in 2019, when I started developing my first project.

After that time, I thought for a long time what is Creagoo is a team or individual developer.

For a while, the websites mentioned "we". It looked weird because I was alone. As a result, I decided that Creagoo is an individual developer.

What does "Creagoo" mean

Creagoo is an abbreviation of the phrase "Create a good". Means "do well."

The slogan may seem obvious, but it accurately defined the further development of Creagoo.

Commerce, advertising

For all the time in Creagoo projects and on the website not a single advertisement was placed.

This is due to Creagoo's policy: Don't talk about offers that I would not use myself.

This means that any advertising on the site or games — this is what I would use myself.

This approach helps to sort the incoming offers perfectly advertising bookmakers, casinos and betting. By the way, such proposals have already been received.
